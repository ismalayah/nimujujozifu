Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: Mutants In Manhattan Gets New Gameplay Trailer

Publish date: 2024-07-23

Activision have released a new gameplay trailer for their upcoming all-action game, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: Mutants in Manhattan. The clip comes just a month after the first announcement trailer for the game dropped, and it shows off some daunting boss battles for the heroes in a half-shell.

Recommended Videos

Developed by Platinum Games, this new Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles adventure will mix the developer’s trademark energetic style with all of the attitude of Nickelodeon’s warriors for an original story by series author Tom Waltz. Featuring a neat 3D style that hearkens to the comic origins, the game will look like it’s come straight from the classic pages.

Mutants in Manhattan will include online co-op play for up to four players, meaning you can team up with friends to complete the gang and battle in the brawls side-by-side. Each of the turtles will feature a distinct playing style, allowing you to experience a different approach depending on which hero you opt for at any time.

The new trailer also reveals that many classic Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles villains will be appearing in the game, with the likes of Shredder, Bebop, Rocksteady, General Krang, the ninja Karai, mutant-alien bat Wingnut, and Slash all featuring in the footage.

Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: Mutants in Manhattan is expected to arrive on May 27th, and it will be available for PS4, PS3, Xbox One, Xbox 360 and PC.
