Spicy Extract (Japanese: ハバネロエキス Habanero Extract) is a non-damaging Grass-type move introduced in Generation IX. It is the signature move of Scovillain.

Effect

Spicy Extract raises the target's Attack and lowers the target's Defense, each by two stages. It bypasses accuracy checks to always hit, unless the target is in the semi-invulnerable turn of a move such as Dig or Fly.
