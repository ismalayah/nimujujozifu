Barbora Zdanska Missing: Amber Alert Issued For A Teenager

Publish date: 2024-08-20

The Barbora Zdanska missing case from Volusia County, Florida, has gained significant media coverage, leading to the activation of an Amber Alert for the 14-year-old girl. 

Barbora was last spotted in Daytona Beach, Florida, dressed in a black Adidas sweatshirt and black jean shorts. 

It is possible that a family member or boyfriend accompanies her in a 2014 blue Dodge Caravan with Florida license plate CZ8613.

The vehicle has distinctive markings, including a butterfly sticker in the top right rear window and a University of Florida “F” sticker in the top left window.

Let’s explore essential details and facts about the Barbora Zdanska Missing case, including an Amber Alert, descriptions of the missing teenager and the vehicle she may be traveling in.

Background

Barbora Zdanska missing raises cause for concern, with reports suggesting that she may have been kidnapped. 

The Daytona Beach Police Department has been working ardently with law enforcement agencies in the region to locate her. 

Speaking to the media, Sgt. Tim Ehrenkaufer confirmed that Barbora was noted getting into a vehicle voluntarily, later revealing that the vehicle’s owner is her boyfriend’s family member. 

Subsequently, Barbora was able to communicate to a family member, indicating that she had been kidnapped. Hence, the authorities issued an Amber Alert.

Description of Barbora Zdanska

Barbora Zdanska is a 14-year-old white female. She has long, blonde hair and blue eyes, stands at 5 feet 5 inches tall, and weighs approximately 136 pounds. 

At the time of her disappearance, Barbora was wearing a black Adidas sweatshirt with white stripes on the sleeves and black jean shorts.

Description of the suspect vehicle

The vehicle Barbora may be traveling in is a 2014 blue Dodge Caravan. It has a Florida license plate bearing CZ8613. 

The vehicle has a butterfly sticker in the top right rear window and a University of Florida “F” sticker in the top left window.

The Investigation So Far

The Daytona Ocean side Police Division has provided details about the potential suspect in Barbora’s case.

The suspect is a slender white man in his mid- to late 20s, standing between 5 feet 7 and 5 feet 9 inches tall.

Although the police have not released the suspect’s identity, they are actively searching for Barbora.

Despite a week passing since Barbora went missing, the investigation is ongoing. The authorities are urging the public to provide any relevant information.

If you have seen Barbora Zdanska or the suspect vehicle in your area, please immediately contact the Daytona Ocean side Police Division at 386-671-5100 or dial 911.

The police department assures anyone with information that their tip can be anonymous, and any leads generated will be followed up without delay.

Moving forward

The Barbora Zdanska missing case is a grave matter, with her safety being of utmost importance. Those with information are urged to assist in the search.

The Daytona Ocean side Police Division is actively seeking to locate Barbora and apprehend the individual responsible.

Authorities are diligently conducting their investigation to ensure Barbora’s safe return to her loved ones.

If anyone has any information, it is imperative to contact the authorities without delay.
