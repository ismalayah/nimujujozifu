Porygon-Z (Japanese: ポリゴンZ Porygon-Z) is a Colorless-type Stage 2 Pokémon card. It is part of the Paradox Rift expansion.

Card text

Buggy Turbo
バグターボ
 
Once during your turn, you may flip a coin. If heads, attach up to 4 Basic Energy cards from your discard pile to this Pokémon. If tails, discard an Energy from this Pokémon.
Energized Attack
エナジーアタック		40×
This attack does 40 damage for each Energy attached to this Pokémon.

Release information

This card was included as a Regular card and a Full Art illustration rare card in the Paradox Rift expansion, first released in the Japanese Future Flash expansion. The Regular print features artwork by hatachu, while the Full Art illustration rare print features artwork by GOSSAN.
